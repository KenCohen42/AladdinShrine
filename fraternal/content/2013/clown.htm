To entertain children of all ages. Members don the colorful makeup and outfits of traditional clowns, to bring a bit more laughter into the world. The Clowns entertain at Shriners Children's Hospitals, march in Shrine parades and perform for a variety of audiences at the discretion of the Potentate.
